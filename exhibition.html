About the Exhibition

"Forbidden Curiosities": A Temporary Exhibition

This temporary art exhibition presents a provocative collection of objects that have stirred debate, challenged societal norms, or had unexpected cultural impacts. By showcasing these items, the exhibition invites visitors to explore how controversy can drive change, spark innovation, and redefine values. Each object tells a story of disruption, inspiring reflection and dialogue.

Highlights Extract:

  • groundbreaking scientific creation that redefined the boundaries of life and technology, raising profound ethical questions.
  • An early astronomical instrument that revolutionized humanity's understanding of its place in the universe, igniting clashes between science and belief.
  • A publication that provoked a cultural shift, testing the limits of freedom of expression and redefining popular media.
  • A contemporary artwork exploring the relationship between mortality and materialism, stirring debate on the value and purpose of art itself.

Location: Palazzo del Portico Nuovo

Nestled in the heart of Bologna, Palazzo del Portico Nuovo stands as a newly revitalized historical gem. Inspired by the city's rich architectural heritage, this fictional palazzo is located on Via dei Marmi, a cobblestone street named for its ancient connection to marble artisans. The palace, once a private residence of a prominent family in the 18th century, has been reimagined as a cultural space dedicated to sparking dialogue on art, history, and society.

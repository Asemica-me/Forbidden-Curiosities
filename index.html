BEYOND BOUNDARIES

This temporary art exhibition presents a provocative collection of objects that have stirred debate, challenged societal norms, or had unexpected cultural impacts. By showcasing these items, the exhibition invites visitors to explore how controversy can drive change, spark innovation, and redefine values. Each object tells a story of disruption, inspiring reflection and dialogue.

DISCOVER THE EXHIBITION

The exhibition delve into items that stirred debate, challenged norms, or had unexpected impacts, offering a provocative look at how controversy can drive change.  Whether you're exploring the scientific revolutions sparked by Galileo's Telescope or contemplating the cultural provocations of Gustave Courbet’s "Origin du monde", this application is your gateway to discovering the stories behind each artifact, unravelling their roles in shaping our past and present.

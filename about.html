ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project explores artifacts that sparked debates, challenged conventions, or had unforeseen impacts, offering a thought-provoking perspective on how controversy fuels change. From the scientific breakthroughs ignited by Galileo's Telescope to the cultural shockwaves triggered by Shoot by Chris Burden, this application invites you to uncover the stories behind these influential pieces, revealing their roles in shaping history and society. With this digital companion, visitors embark on a journey through time, discovering, learning, and reflecting on the controversies and innovations that have defined our world.
